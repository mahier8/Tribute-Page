Trent Alexander Arnold

He took the corner quickly... and the rest was history.

Here's a look at some of TAA's achievements so far:

  • 2017 - Alexander-Arnold made more than 100 appearances for the club in just over three seasons after his debut.
  • 2019 - Won the UEFA Champions League during the season of 2018-19 with Liverpool FC.
  • 2019 - His ingenious, quickly taken corner to set-up Divock Origi to score the winning goal in front of the Kop propelled Liverpool to the final.
  • 2019 - Won the 2019 UEFA Super Cup in Istanbul, Turkey, where Liverpool FC defeated Chelsea.
  • 2019 - Won the World Club Cup in Qatar, where Liverpool FC overcame Monterrey and Flamengo.
  • 2020 - Won the English Premier League title with a record of seven games to spare, while breaking his own record for the amount of assists by a defender.

“He has the right attitude and comes from West Derby, home to Melwood. So Trent is another Scouser and apparently, just as I tried to be John Barnes and Steve McMahon, he grew up pretending to be me while playing in the Merseyside parks."

-Steven Gerrard
